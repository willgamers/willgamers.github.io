Assassin's Creed Freerunners

<h2> Description </h2> <p> In <b>Assassin's Creed Freerunners</b>, you step into the shoes of your chosen assassin and compete against seven others in a thrilling freerunning race to the extraction point. Traverse daunting environments, from scaling rooftops to leaping walls. Each action performed is aimed at maximizing your synchronization, enabling you to summon famed characters from the Assassin's Creed franchise. It's an epic race, and only the quickest and nimblest will survive. </p> <h2> How to play </h2> <p> You control your character using intuitive on-screen controls. Run by swiping left or right, climb or descend with up/downward swipes and perform jumps or flips with specified gestures. Be prepared for split-second decisions as your route is filled with unforeseen obstacles. To progress in the game, increase your synchronization level by performing stunts and finish first in races which unlocks experienced assassins. </p> <h2> Tips and Tricks </h2> <p> The key to success is <b>speed and efficiency</b>. Plan your route ahead and avoid unnecessary risks that may slow you down. Make use of the environment, every high jump increases synchronization faster. Always be on guard for sudden obstacles; quick reflexes could mean the difference between victory and defeat. </p> <h2> Features </h2> <ul> <li>Thrilling freerunning gameplay based on popular Assassin's Creed franchise. </li> <li>Compete against 7 other players in real-time races. </li> <li>Increase synchronization to summon legendary characters from Assassin's Creed series. </li> <li>Epic environments offering endless parkour possibilities. </li> <li>Interactive multiplayer mode promoting competitive gameplay. </li> </ul>
